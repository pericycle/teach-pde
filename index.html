Description

Introduction to the subject of Partial Differential Equations. Topics include classification of PDEs, first-order linear and non-linear PDEs, second order PDEs including the heat, wave and Laplace's equation. Solution methods such as the method of characteristics for hyberbolic equations, separation of variables and eigenfunction expansions, Laplace and Fourier transforms.

Textbooks
  • Introduction to Partial Differential Equations, 2nd edition, by Walter Strauss.
  • Partial Differential Equations in Action, From Modelling to Theory, 3rd edition, by Sandro Salsa.
  • Applied Partial Differential Equations, 3rd edition, by J. David Logan.
  • Applied Partial Differential Equations, An Introduction, by Alan Jeffrey.
